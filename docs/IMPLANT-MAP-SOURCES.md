# Implant Map — per-sentence source ledger

Private record tracing **every sentence** of the Maps-tab "Implant" pathway
(single-tooth, two-stage, restorative) to a specific source slide. The deployed
prose is de-identified and citation-free; this ledger is where the attribution
lives. The discipline here is the owner's explicit rule: *every sentence cited to
a specific slide — do not get loose, do not imply a slide "said" something it
only loosely implied.*

## Source

- **[Yuan]** — "Clinical Procedures for Single Tooth Implant Restorations,"
  Judy Chia-Chun Yuan, DDS, MS, FACP (predoctoral STI, wk7). The slide deck,
  exported to text at `~/Documents/Dentistry Files/New Files/Exam Stuff/
  Lecture_Text/DAOB Lectures/Implantology/UIC Predoctoral STI wk7.txt`. Line
  numbers below are lines in that file; each line range is one slide (or one
  bullet on a slide). This deck is the spine of the whole Map — it carries the
  assessment checklist, the surgical protocol, and the full restorative sequence.
- Corroborating (not separately cited in the Map, already in
  `IMPLANT-ENGINE-SOURCES.md`): **[TBL]** clinic checklist, **[Han]** OMFS
  anatomic minimums. Every checklist threshold the Map prints is also printed on
  a Yuan slide, so the Map cites Yuan.

name, room numbers (Implant Clinic Room 321, Advanced Prosthodontic Clinic Room

implant program" and "advanced prosthodontic clinic" are kept lowercase generic.
Brand names kept (Astra/Osseospeed/EV, Straumann, Dentsply, Atlantis, e.max,
Silky Rock, Accufilm, ShimStock) — they are product facts, not identifiers.

## Discipline notes (what was NOT written, and why)

- **No peri-implantitis rationale.** Slide L820-836 (Cementation) says only "remove
  all excess cement (verify w/ radiograph)." It does not state *why*. The very
  common teaching "retained cement → peri-implantitis" (Wilson 2009) is real but is
  **not on a cited slide**, so it was cut from the delivery chapter and from the
  pathway `keyDecisions`. The Map states the instruction, not an uncited rationale.
- **Occlusion wording kept literal to the slide.** L744-757 pairs Accufilm (40 µm,
  marks on firm closure) and ShimStock (8 µm, passes under light closure). The Map
  reproduces that pairing and does not add the broader "leave the implant crown in
  infraocclusion" generalization, which this deck does not state in those words.
- **"~48 hours after the order" was dropped** from the Map prose even though it is on
  L374 — it is a logistics detail tied to the named staff/clinic and adds nothing to
  the workflow once "request two weeks ahead" (L288-289) is stated.
- **Codes/fees (L164-215) intentionally omitted.** The CDT codes live in the app's
  Codes/Steps tabs; the Map is the clinical workflow, so the fee table was not
  transcribed (avoids a wall of numbers the schematic can't render).
